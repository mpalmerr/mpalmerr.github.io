Bio

I am a PhD student in clinical neuropsychology at Université de Montréal, based at the International Laboratory for Brain, Music and Sound Research (BRAMS), under the supervision of Isabelle Peretz. My doctoral research examines the relationship between cardiac dynamics and musical beat processing, exploring how physiological signals may interact with beat perception and sensorimotor synchronization.

Research interests

Music cognition · Beat perception · Sensorimotor synchronization · Cardiac dynamics · Clinical neuropsychology · Beat deafness · Aging

Beyond research

Alongside my academic work, I maintain a creative practice as a musician, producer, and singer. Before beginning my university studies, I worked professionally as a recording artist and composer, and I am currently writing a second album as an independent artist.
